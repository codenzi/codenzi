Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 14, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Smart Archive, provided by Codenzi ("we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("Service").

Core Principle: You Control Your Data. The files, documents, and media you save in Smart Archive ("User Content") are stored locally on your device. The optional Google Drive backup feature saves your User Content to your own Google Drive account, which we do not have access to. Your privacy and the security of your content are paramount.

2. Information We Collect

To provide and improve our Service, we collect the following types of information:

Type of Information Source Purpose
User Content (Files, Images, etc.) Provided directly by you Stored locally on your device for core app functionality. We do not access, view, or upload this content to our servers.
Technical and Usage Data Collected automatically via Firebase Analytics Anonymous data such as device type, OS version, feature usage frequency, and general location (e.g., country). This helps us understand user behavior to improve the app. This data is aggregated and does not personally identify you.
Crash and Performance Data Collected automatically via Firebase Crashlytics Technical information about app crashes and performance issues, including device state at the time of a crash. This helps us diagnose and fix bugs.
Advertising Data Collected automatically via Google AdMob Device identifiers (like a mobile advertising ID) and interaction data with ads. This is used to serve relevant ads and support the Service financially.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To Provide and Maintain the Service: To enable you to store, manage, and back up your User Content.
  • To Improve the Service: We analyze anonymous usage and performance data to identify trends, fix bugs, and enhance features.
  • To Support the Service Financially: We display advertisements through Google AdMob, which helps keep the core features of the app free.
  • To Secure the Service: To prevent fraud and ensure compliance with our Terms of Use.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We only share data with trusted third-party service providers as necessary to operate the Service. You are in control of any sharing you initiate.

  • With Google (as a Service Provider): We share technical data with Google for the purposes of analytics (Firebase), crash reporting (Crashlytics), and advertising (AdMob). Google's use of this data is governed by their own privacy policies.
  • With Your Own Google Drive Account: When you initiate a backup, you authorize the app to transfer your User Content to your own Google Drive account. This is a transfer you control, and we have no visibility or liability regarding the data once it is in your Drive.
  • For Legal Reasons: We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with a legal obligation, protect our rights, or prevent fraud.

5. Data Storage, Security, and Retention

Data Storage

Your User Content is stored exclusively in two places, both under your control:

  1. On Your Device: Within the application's private, sandboxed storage area.
  2. In Your Google Drive Account: If you use the optional backup feature.

We do not have a copy of your User Content on our servers.

Security

We implement reasonable technical measures to protect the information processed by the app. However, please be aware that no security measures are perfect or impenetrable. You are responsible for the security of your device and your Google account credentials.

Data Retention

We retain technical data (analytics, crash reports) for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. You can delete your User Content from your device and your Google Drive at any time.

6. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for use by children under the age of 13, or the equivalent minimum age in the relevant jurisdiction. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy within the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Additional Information for Users in the European Union (EU), EEA, and UK

If you are located in the European Union, European Economic Area, or the United Kingdom, you have certain rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Legal Basis for Processing

Our legal basis for collecting and using the personal information described above will depend on the information concerned and the context in which we collect it. We process data on the following bases:

  • Consent: For processing data for personalized advertising. We use Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) to obtain this consent. You can withdraw your consent at any time in the app's settings.
  • Legitimate Interests: For processing anonymous analytics and crash data to improve our Service, provided that such processing does not override your rights and freedoms.

Your Data Protection Rights

You have the following rights:

  • The right to access, update, or delete the information we have on you.
  • The right of rectification.
  • The right to object to our processing.
  • The right of restriction.
  • The right to data portability.
  • The right to withdraw consent at any time.

Since your primary data (User Content) is on your device or your Google Drive, you have direct control over it. For data related to analytics or advertising identifiers, please contact us.

Your California Privacy Rights (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), provides you with specific rights regarding your personal information.

  • Right to Know: You can request to know what personal information we collect, use, and disclose.
  • Right to Delete: You can request the deletion of your personal information. As your User Content is stored locally or on your own Google Drive, you can directly delete it.
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale/Sharing: We do not sell your data. However, our use of Google AdMob for personalized ads may constitute "sharing" under CPRA. You can opt-out of this sharing via the privacy choices presented in the app.
  • Right to Correct: You have the right to request the correction of inaccurate personal information.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights.

Türkiye'deki Kullanıcılar için Ek Bilgiler (KVKK Aydınlatma Metni)

Bu bölüm, 6698 sayılı Kişisel Verilerin Korunması Kanunu ("KVKK") uyarınca Türkiye'de ikamet eden kullanıcılarımızı bilgilendirmek amacıyla hazırlanmıştır.

Veri Sorumlusu

KVKK kapsamında, kişisel verilerinizle ilgili olarak Veri Sorumlusu Codenzi'dir.

İşlenen Kişisel Veriler, İşleme Amaçları ve Hukuki Sebepler

Uygulamamızda işlenen veriler, amaçları ve hukuki sebepleri aşağıda detaylandırılmıştır:

  • Kullanıcı İçerikleri: Tarafınızca uygulamaya yüklenen dosya ve belgelerdir. Bu veriler yalnızca cihazınızda ve talimatınız üzerine kendi Google Drive hesabınızda saklanır. İşleme hukuki sebebi, KVKK Madde 5/2(c) uyarınca "bir sözleşmenin kurulması veya ifasıyla doğrudan doğruya ilgili olması" ve "veri işlemenin zorunlu olmasıdır." Biz bu içeriğe erişmeyiz.
  • Teknik, Reklam ve Analitik Veriler: Cihaz kimlikleri, çökme raporları ve anonim kullanım istatistikleridir. Bu veriler; hizmeti iyileştirmek, hataları gidermek ve reklam sunarak hizmeti finanse etmek amacıyla, KVKK Madde 5/2(f) uyarınca "ilgili kişinin temel hak ve özgürlüklerine zarar vermemek kaydıyla, veri sorumlusunun meşru menfaatleri için veri işlenmesinin zorunlu olması" ve kişiselleştirilmiş reklamlar için KVKK Madde 5/1 uyarınca "açık rızanız" hukuki sebeplerine dayalı olarak işlenir.

Kişisel Verilerin Aktarımı

Teknik verileriniz, hizmet aldığımız Google'ın yurt dışındaki (örn. Amerika Birleşik Devletleri) sunucularına aktarılmaktadır. Bu aktarım, açık rızanız veya KVKK'nın 9. maddesinde belirtilen şartlar dahilinde gerçekleştirilir.

KVKK Kapsamındaki Haklarınız

KVKK'nın 11. maddesi uyarınca, veri sorumlusuna başvurarak; kişisel verilerinizin işlenip işlenmediğini öğrenme, işlenmişse bilgi talep etme, işlenme amacını ve uygunluğunu öğrenme, verilerin aktarıldığı üçüncü kişileri bilme, düzeltilmesini isteme, silinmesini veya yok edilmesini isteme, bu işlemlerin üçüncü kişilere bildirilmesini isteme, otomatik sistemlerle analiz edilmesiyle aleyhinize bir sonucun ortaya çıkmasına itiraz etme ve kanuna aykırı işlenme sebebiyle zarara uğramanız hâlinde zararın giderilmesini talep etme haklarına sahipsiniz.
