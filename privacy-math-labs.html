1. Introduction and Scope

Welcome to MathLabs. This Privacy Policy is a legally binding agreement between you and Codenzi Labs ("we," "us," or "our"). It governs the collection, use, processing, and disclosure of your Personal Data when you download, install, access, or use our mobile application, MathLabs (the "Application").

This Policy is designed to comply with major privacy regulations, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and the Turkish Law on the Protection of Personal Data (KVKK). By using the Application, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Data Controller: For the purposes of GDPR and KVKK, the data controller is Codenzi Labs. Contact details are provided in Section 12.

2. Data We Collect and Process

We collect information that is essential for providing our services, improving your experience, and fulfilling our legal obligations. The data is categorized as follows:

2.1. Data You Directly Provide

  • Account and Identity Data: When you register and sign in using your Google Account, we receive your full name, email address, and a unique, persistent User ID (UID) from Google. This data is fundamental to creating and securing your account.
  • User-Generated Content:
    • AI Assistant Prompts: We process the text, questions, and mathematical problems you submit to our AI Assistant.
    • PDF Annotations and Drawings: Any annotations, highlights, drawings, or notes you create on PDF documents within the Application are collected. This data is primarily stored locally on your device but may be cached on our servers to enable future features.

2.2. Data Collected Automatically (Technical and Usage Data)

  • Service Usage and Interaction Data: We automatically log your activity within the Application. This includes features accessed, session start and end times, duration of use, number of AI queries performed, number of PDF downloads, and interactions with UI elements (e.g., button clicks). This is collected via Google Firebase Analytics.
  • Device and Connection Data: We collect technical information about the device you use, including the device model, operating system name and version, unique device identifiers (e.g., Android ID), IP address, language settings, and mobile network information.
  • Crash and Performance Data: If the Application encounters an error or crashes, we automatically collect diagnostic data via Google Firebase Crashlytics. This includes a detailed crash report (stack trace), the state of the application at the time of the crash, and the device information mentioned above. This data is anonymized and used exclusively for debugging and improving stability.
  • Purchase and Transaction Data: When you subscribe to a premium plan, the transaction is processed by Google Play Billing. We receive a confirmation token from Google, which includes order details like the purchase ID, subscription status (active, canceled), and renewal date. We do not receive, process, or store your credit card or other financial information.
  • Advertising Data: For non-premium users, we collect your device’s Advertising Identifier (e.g., Google Ad ID). This identifier is used by Google AdMob to serve personalized advertisements based on your interests and app usage. You can reset this identifier or opt-out of personalized advertising through your device's settings.

3. Legal Basis and Purposes for Data Processing

We process your personal data based on specific legal grounds as required by GDPR and other applicable laws. Each purpose is tied to a legal basis:

  • Performance of a Contract (GDPR Art. 6(1)(b)): We process your data to fulfill our contractual obligations to you. This includes:
    • Creating and managing your user account.
    • Authenticating your identity and providing secure access.
    • Delivering core services, such as PDF viewing, annotations, and AI-powered problem-solving.
    • Processing your premium subscription and activating premium features.
    • Providing customer support and responding to your inquiries.
  • Legitimate Interests (GDPR Art. 6(1)(f)): We process data for our legitimate business interests, provided they do not override your fundamental rights and freedoms. This includes:
    • Analyzing usage patterns to understand how our Application is used, allowing us to improve features, usability, and overall performance.
    • Monitoring application stability, diagnosing, and fixing bugs and crashes.
    • Securing our services and preventing fraudulent activity.
    • Serving non-personalized ads to non-premium users to support the free version of our service.
  • Consent (GDPR Art. 6(1)(a)): We rely on your explicit consent for certain processing activities:
    • Serving personalized advertisements through Google AdMob. We will request your consent via a consent management platform before collecting advertising identifiers for this purpose, especially for users in the EEA and UK.
    • Sending promotional or marketing communications via email, from which you can opt-out at any time.
  • Compliance with a Legal Obligation (GDPR Art. 6(1)(c)): We may process your data to comply with applicable laws, regulations, or valid legal requests from law enforcement or public authorities.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure (Third-Party Sub-processors)

We do not sell your personal data. We only share data with trusted third-party service providers (sub-processors) who are contractually obligated to protect it. Our key sub-processors are:

  • Google, LLC: As a core technology partner, we use several Google services:
    • Firebase: For user authentication, cloud database (Firestore), backend functions, analytics, and crash reporting. Data shared includes account info, usage data, and device data.
    • Google Generative AI (Gemini API): Your AI prompts are sent to this service for processing to generate responses. Google may use this data as described in their API terms and privacy policy.
    • Google Play Billing: To process and manage your premium subscriptions. Transaction data is shared directly with Google.
    • Google AdMob: To display ads to non-premium users. Your advertising ID is shared for this purpose.
    Data processed by Google is subject to the Google Privacy Policy.
  • Legal and Safety Disclosures: We may disclose your information if we believe it is necessary to (a) comply with a law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request; (b) protect the safety of any person; (c) address fraud, security, or technical issues; or (d) protect our rights or property.

5. Data Security Measures

We implement robust technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit using Transport Layer Security (TLS/HTTPS). Data at rest within Firebase services is also encrypted.
  • Access Control: Access to personal data on our backend systems is strictly limited to authorized personnel who require access to perform their job functions.
  • Secure Development: We follow secure coding practices to prevent vulnerabilities in our Application.
  • Disclaimer: While we take all reasonable steps to secure your data, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected or to comply with legal obligations.

  • Account Data: We retain your account information for as long as your account remains active. If you request account deletion, we will permanently delete your personal data from our production systems within 30 days and from our backup systems within 90 days.
  • Usage and Analytics Data: Aggregated and anonymized analytics data may be retained indefinitely for statistical purposes.
  • Locally Stored Data: Data stored on your device, such as PDF annotations, is deleted when you uninstall the Application.

7. Your Data Protection Rights

Under GDPR, CCPA, and KVKK, you have specific rights regarding your personal data. We are committed to upholding these rights. You have the right to:

  • Access Your Data: You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectify Your Data: You have the right to request the correction of any inaccurate or incomplete personal data. (Note: Your name and email are managed via your Google Account.)
  • Erase Your Data (Right to be Forgotten): You can request the deletion of your account and all associated personal data. This action is irreversible.
  • Restrict Processing: You can request that we restrict the processing of your personal data under certain conditions (e.g., if you contest its accuracy).
  • Data Portability: You can request that we provide you with your data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format, and you have the right to transmit that data to another controller.
  • Object to Processing: You have the right to object to the processing of your data where we are relying on legitimate interests as our legal basis.
  • Withdraw Consent: Where we rely on your consent (e.g., for personalized ads), you have the right to withdraw it at any time, without affecting the lawfulness of processing based on consent before its withdrawal.
  • Lodge a Complaint: You have the right to lodge a complaint with a data protection supervisory authority in your country of residence.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact our Data Protection Officer using the contact details in Section 12.

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 16 (or the applicable age of consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal data without parental consent, we will take steps to delete such information.

Your personal data is processed on servers managed by Google, which are located globally. This means your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure that such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws. For transfers of data outside the EEA, we rely on legal mechanisms such as the European Commission's Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) to ensure your data is adequately protected.

Hizmetlerimizi sunmak, deneyiminizi iyileştirmek ve yasal yükümlülüklerimizi yerine getirmek için gerekli olan bilgileri topluyoruz. Veriler aşağıdaki gibi kategorize edilmiştir:

2.1. Doğrudan Sağladığınız Veriler

  • Hesap ve Kimlik Verileri: Google Hesabınızla kaydolduğunuzda ve oturum açtığınızda, Google'dan tam adınızı, e-posta adresinizi ve benzersiz, kalıcı bir Kullanıcı Kimliği (UID) alırız. Bu veriler, hesabınızı oluşturmak ve güvence altına almak için esastır.
  • Kullanıcı Tarafından Oluşturulan İçerik:
    • Yapay Zeka Asistanı İstekleri: Yapay Zeka Asistanımıza gönderdiğiniz metin, soru ve matematiksel problemleri işleriz.
    • PDF Ek Açıklamaları ve Çizimler: Uygulama içinde PDF belgeleri üzerinde oluşturduğunuz tüm ek açıklamalar, vurgular, çizimler veya notlar toplanır. Bu veriler öncelikli olarak cihazınızda yerel olarak saklanır, ancak gelecekteki özellikleri etkinleştirmek için sunucularımızda önbelleğe alınabilir.

  • Hizmet Kullanım ve Etkileşim Verileri: Uygulama içindeki etkinliğinizi otomatik olarak günlüğe kaydederiz. Bu, erişilen özellikleri, oturum başlangıç ve bitiş zamanlarını, kullanım süresini, gerçekleştirilen yapay zeka sorgularının sayısını, PDF indirme sayısını ve kullanıcı arayüzü öğeleriyle (örn. düğme tıklamaları) etkileşimleri içerir. Bu veriler Google Firebase Analytics aracılığıyla toplanır.
  • Cihaz ve Bağlantı Verileri: Kullandığınız cihaz hakkında cihaz modeli, işletim sistemi adı ve sürümü, benzersiz cihaz tanımlayıcıları (örn. Android ID), IP adresi, dil ayarları ve mobil ağ bilgileri dahil olmak üzere teknik bilgiler toplarız.
  • Çökme ve Performans Verileri: Uygulama bir hatayla karşılaşırsa veya çökerse, Google Firebase Crashlytics aracılığıyla otomatik olarak teşhis verileri toplarız. Bu, ayrıntılı bir çökme raporu (stack trace), çökme anında uygulamanın durumu ve yukarıda belirtilen cihaz bilgilerini içerir. Bu veriler anonimleştirilir ve yalnızca hata ayıklama ve kararlılığı artırma amacıyla kullanılır.

Google'dan sipariş kimliği, abonelik durumu (aktif, iptal edilmiş) ve yenileme tarihi gibi sipariş ayrıntılarını içeren bir onay belirteci alırız. Kredi kartı veya diğer finansal bilgilerinizi almayız, işlemeyiz veya saklamayız.
  • Reklamcılık Verileri: Premium olmayan kullanıcılar için, cihazınızın Reklam Tanımlayıcısını (örn. Google Reklam Kimliği) toplarız. Bu tanımlayıcı, ilgi alanlarınıza ve uygulama kullanımınıza göre kişiselleştirilmiş reklamlar sunmak için Google AdMob tarafından kullanılır.
