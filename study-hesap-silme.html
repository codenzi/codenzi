Account & Data Deletion Request

At StudiCat (Codenzi), we value your privacy. In compliance with Google Play policies, you can request the deletion of your account and all associated data.

What Data Will Be Deleted?
  • Personal Information (Name, email, profile picture)
  • In-app Data (Solved questions, statistics, progress)
  • Device & Diagnostic Data (Anonymous identifiers collected for analytics)
Process and Retention Period

You can submit your request by clicking the button below. After identity verification, all your data will be permanently cleared from our servers within 30 days. Data that must be kept for legal obligations (e.g., invoices) will be anonymized after this period.
